# Project Name and Positioning

## Primary name

**MacroFair**

## Expanded title

**MacroFair: A Live Fair-Value Engine for Macro Prediction Markets**

## Why this name works

- Short and memorable
- Signals macro focus immediately
- Suggests fair value / calibration rather than hype trading
- Serious enough for judges, clear enough for demo viewers

## One-line positioning

MacroFair detects when macro prediction-market prices diverge from fundamentals, ranks the most interesting dislocations, and explains the gap.

## Two-line elevator pitch

Prediction markets are useful, but they are not always calibrated.  
MacroFair compares live Polymarket and Kalshi contract prices against a fundamentals-aware fair-value model, then surfaces the biggest dislocations with confidence scores and explanations.
